NWD collects and processes your personal and health information solely for the purpose of delivering diagnostic services, providing online access to your results, and facilitating appointment management.

Your records are retained for a period consistent with applicable medical and legal requirements. Result access via this portal is limited to the most recent 3–6 months.

Help & Frequently Asked Questions
How do I log in to the Patient Portal? ▾
Use your Patient ID (found on your NWD receipt or health card) and your Date of Birth as the password. No registration is required.
When will my results be available? ▾
Laboratory results are typically available within 4–8 hours after your visit. Imaging results (X-ray, ultrasound) may take 24–48 hours. You will receive an SMS/email notification when results are ready.
How long are my results available on the portal? ▾
Results from the past 3–6 months are accessible via the portal. For older records, please visit any NWD branch or contact your home branch directly.
Can I change my login password? ▾
Your current password is your Date of Birth. Password change functionality is not yet available in Phase 1. This will be introduced in a future update.
Why can't I see my Medical Evaluation results? ▾
Medical evaluation results may require physician clearance before they are released online. Please contact your attending physician or visit your NWD branch to request access.
Is my health data safe? ▾
Yes. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Your session automatically expires after 15 minutes of inactivity. NWD complies fully with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). View our full Privacy Notice at login.
How do I share results with my doctor? ▾
Go to Share Results in the sidebar. Generate a secure, time-limited link and send it to your physician. No login is required on their end. Links expire automatically after 7 days.
I need help — who do I contact? ▾
Contact your nearest NWD branch or email us at support@nwdi.com.ph. You may also call our hotline at (02) 8XXX-XXXX during business hours (Mon–Sat, 7AM–6PM).
