Authentication 2 features
#FeatureDescription
1 HR Login HR Only /hr/login Dedicated HR staff login page with NWDI branding. Staff sign in using their registered email and password. On success, automatically redirected to /hr/dashboard. Patients who attempt to log in here are redirected to the patient portal.
2 Role-Based Redirect HR Only Middleware Middleware enforces role separation. HR/Admin accounts are always routed to /hr/dashboard after login. Patient accounts cannot access any /hr/* route and are redirected to their own dashboard. All HR routes require authenticated HR/Admin role.

HR Dashboard 5 features
#FeatureDescription
3 Hospital Stats Overview /hr/dashboard 4 stat cards showing hospital-wide totals: Total Active Patients (all registered), Today's Visits (since midnight), Total Visits (all time), and Pending Results (awaiting release).
4 Recent Visits Feed /hr/dashboard Live feed of the last 5 hospital visits across all patients. Shows patient name, queue code, patient type, station, visit status badge, and date. Links to the full visits list.
5 Top Patients by Visit Count /hr/dashboard Ranked list of the top 5 patients with the most visits hospital-wide. Shows patient name, patient code, and total visit count. Clicking a patient navigates to their full detail page.
6 Welcome Banner /hr/dashboard Time-based greeting with the HR staff member's name. Identifies the user as HR staff.
7 Quick Navigation Links /hr/dashboard 4 quick-access cards linking directly to: Browse Employees, View All Visits, Lab Results, and Payments — for fast navigation from the dashboard.

Employees / Patient Records 5 features
#FeatureDescription
8 All Employees List All Records /hr/employees Paginated table of every registered patient/employee in the hospital system. Shows 20 per page. Displays: full name, patient code, gender, age, contact, active status, and last visit date.
9 Employee Search /hr/employees Search across all patients by: full name, first name, last name, patient code, email, or mobile number. Results update with debounce as the user types.
10 Filter by Gender & Status /hr/employees Pill-button filters to narrow the list by: Gender (All / Male / Female) and Account Status (All / Active / Inactive). Filters can be combined and cleared with one click.
11 Employee / Patient Detail /hr/employees/[code] Full profile page for any individual patient. Shows: personal info (name, DOB, gender, email, mobile, address), latest vital signs snapshot, complete visit history, all lab/imaging results, and full payment history — all in one view.
12 Latest Vitals Snapshot /hr/employees/[code] On the employee detail page, displays the most recent recorded vitals: Blood Pressure, Heart Rate, Temperature, Weight, Height, BMI, and Chief Complaint — pulled from their latest visit with vital signs recorded.

All Visits 3 features
#FeatureDescription
13 Hospital-Wide Visit List All Records /hr/visits Paginated table of all visits across every patient in the hospital. Shows 25 per page. Displays: patient name, queue code, patient type, station, date/time, and visit status.
14 Visit Search /hr/visits Search all visits by patient name (full, first, or last) or queue code. Debounced live search.
15 Filter by Visit Status /hr/visits Filter visits by status: All, Waiting, In Progress, Done, or Cancelled. Pill-style buttons with one-click clear.

Lab Results 3 features
#FeatureDescription
16 All Lab Results List All Records /hr/results Paginated table of all lab and imaging accessions across all patients. Shows 25 per page. Displays: test description, accession number, item group, type, date, and release status.
17 Result Search /hr/results Search across all results by: test description, accession number, item group, or queue code.
18 Filter by Release Status /hr/results Filter results by online release status: All, Released, or Pending. Color-coded status badges (green = Released, amber = Pending).

Vital Signs 3 features
#FeatureDescription
19 All Vitals List All Records /hr/vitals Card-based view of all recorded vital sign entries across all patients, ordered by most recent. Shows 25 per page.
20 Per-Patient Vitals Card /hr/vitals Each vital record is displayed as a card showing: patient name, queue code, date, Blood Pressure, Heart Rate, Temperature, Weight, and Chief Complaint.
21 Vitals on Employee Detail /hr/employees/[code] Within each patient's detail page, the most recent vitals are shown as a snapshot panel — Blood Pressure, Heart Rate, Temperature, Weight, Height, BMI, and Chief Complaint with the attending doctor's name.

Payments 4 features
#FeatureDescription
22 All Payments List All Records /hr/payments Paginated table of all payment transactions across all patients. Shows 25 per page. Displays: patient name, OR number, payment type, coverage type, amount, discount, date, and status.
23 Payment Search /hr/payments Search all payments by OR number or cashier name.
24 Filter by Payment Type /hr/payments Filter payments by method: All, Cash, GCash, Maya, Card, HMO, or PhilHealth. Pill-style buttons.
